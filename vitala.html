PROBLEM STATEMENT

“Take care of your body. It’s the only place you have to live.”

Let’s start with a motivational quote by Jim Rohn that says: “Take care of your body. It’s the only place you have to live.” Why is this statement so revelational? Nowadays, we are forced to live in a very fast-paced world that drains us immensely. We are taught that things are easily replaceable, however, we cannot purchase a new body to keep up with this rapid pace. We must cherish the body what we already have and try to fix it by fueling it properly.

SOLUTION

Disrupting old eating habits

The solution is a recipe website that provides continuous help in disrupting people’s old eating habits and creating new ones that will improve their overall health. In summary, by following the various instructions, the targeted users will be able to adapt faster and gain confidence in their capabilities to create a well-balanced diet in the long run according to their needs.

GOALS

Our users’ main achievements thanks to the web app:

  • Acquiring advanced cooking skills
  • Being in control of their health
  • Sharing their health journey

RESEARCH & ANALYSIS

Objectives

Who?

People who need guidance to learn cooking techniques and methods in order to eat healthy or keep a specific diet.

When?

The website will be used before/during purchasing groceries and kitchen tools and during/after cooking the dishes.

What?

The target users will be able to find/ save/share recipes, to follow preparation instructions, to get meal plans/grocery list of each recipe/kitchen tips and to adjust recipes according to user preferences.

Why?

Committing to a new lifestyle is a hard task to do, therefore this website would be an optimal choice for those who want to go through this adjustment phase easier and more efficiently.

Where?

The recipe website will be used in stores and in the kitchen of the users.​

How?

By selecting their preferences like a specific diet, an allergen, portion size or kitchen measurement, users can choose from the recommended meal plans, then they can go grocery shopping and cook the customized recipes in their home.

TAKEAWAYS

Reflection on the project

My goal was to ease the pain which comes with the attempt of following given instructions in recipe web apps in general. In my opinion, I met the student project’s objective since I created a design that allows the target users to satisfy their basic needs (find/save/share recipes and to follow preparation instructions).

Roadblocks

I had some struggles with finding the optimal color palette. However, focusing on the vision made me realize that simplicity is the key in this project. According to this, I switched to another approach, and I changed my brighter color palette to a more toned down color palette. Thanks to the change, the imagery of the web app can have more emphasis.

Direction changes

I did not make major direction changes in this project. The only addition I made that I created the concept of an AI assistant (FlavorMate) in the app to make room for more potential features in the following iterations.

Next iteration

I would add the following features in the next iteration:

  • Search filters
  • Recipe recommendations/meal plans
  • Shopping list
  • Adjusting portion size/kitchen measurement
  • Cooking mode
