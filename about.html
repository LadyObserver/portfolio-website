About Me

My Story

I am Nikolett, a UI designer born and bred in Hungary but based in the U.S. as a naturalized U.S. citizen.

As a small girl from a tiny Hungarian village, I had never imagined myself once living in a town, and especially not in New York City! However, I never liked the conventional ways of living. Therefore, I did a couple of first things in my family like being a first-generation college degree holder and becoming a first-generation American.

My journey to becoming a UI designer was not a straightforward one. For the matter of my education, firstly, I acquired my bachelor's degree majoring in retail and marketing from Budapest Business School. After working for several years in the retail and hospitality industry and having some entrepreneurial experience, I started to show interest in computer science, especially coding. To fulfill my newly found dream, I applied for a computer science master's program - Master of Science in Information Systems - at the New Jersey Institute of Technology. During my studies, I became fond of visual design, user experience design, and front-end development. After getting my masterâ€™s, my path has been crystalized for me which is UI design.

Beyond my professional life, I am an avid walker; I can walk 10000 steps daily with a smile on my face! After these long walks, I like to cook or read a book about behavioral economics, popular science, design, or history. An oddity of my character is that as a secular person, one of my favorite activities is to visit any religious sites on my sight during my travels.
