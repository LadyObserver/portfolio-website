BRAND CONCEPT

Motherhood as transformation

The name Momsoon combines monsoon and mom soon, drawing on the symbolism of transformation. Like a monsoon that reshapes the landscape and brings new life, motherhood is a period of intense change. Though pregnancy and childbirth can be turbulent, they are followed by recovery, growth, and renewed strength.

GOAL

Guiding the journey

Momsoon is a maternity and baby clothing platform designed to support mothers-to-be throughout pregnancy and postpartum. Its mission is to help women navigate the transition into motherhood with confidence by offering comfortable clothing and a supportive community that accompanies them at every stage of the journey.

APPROACH

Intentional design

To encourage women to engage with the website, Momsoon uses a palette of pastel pink, soft gold, lilac, and mint green to create a sense of calm and optimism. The typefaces Amiri and Lato reinforce a concise and supportive tone of voice. Diverse and intimate imagery reflects the brand’s commitment to inclusivity and vulnerability.

USER STORIES & KEY FEATURES

Designing around real needs

What is on sale?

“As a customer, I want to be able to see when stock is on sale so that I can save money.”

Let me have a look without committing

As a new customer, I want to access the inventory without having to register so that I can make sure this store has what I’m looking for before having to create an account.”

I like it; I might buy it!

“As a returning customer, I want to be able to save items that I cannot buy to a wish list so that I can purchase them at a later date.”

Moms have no time to waste

“As a customer, I want to have access to advanced filtering options so that I don’t have to browse through a lot of products to find what I’m looking for.”

Growth spurts are no joke!

“As a customer, I want to shop somewhere with a flexible returns policy and good customer service so that I can easily return what I do not want.”

Two heads are better than one

“As a customer, I want to see what other customers have said about products and how they have rated them so I can better judge whether the item is right for me.”

Any recommendations?

“As a frequent customer, I want to see recommendations of products I may like so that I find items I might not otherwise on my own.”

TAKEAWAYS

Lessons learned

Letting go of early ideas during wireframing can be challenging, but exploring alternatives is essential for refining a strong layout. Recreating my sketches in Balsamiq allowed me to quickly experiment with multiple versions and iterate efficiently. This flexibility made the transition from low- to mid-fidelity wireframes smooth and purposeful.

Selecting an effective color palette is often time-consuming. To streamline this process, I used AI-generated suggestions as a starting point and evaluated them with the Realtime Colors tool. Combining these digital tools helped me make more confident design decisions and improved the efficiency of my workflow.

Next iteration

In future iterations, I would expand the platform with features that strengthen usability and community engagement:

  • Multiple payment options
  • Purchase history tracking
  • A space for sharing personal stories
  • Clothing swap and donation features
